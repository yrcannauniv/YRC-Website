Events By YRC (CEG CAMPUS)

UYIRTHULI (Blood Donation Camp)


Uyirthuli is a blood donation camp conducted by Youth Red Cross - CEG every year. 20 minutes of our time and 250 cc of our blood make the huge difference between life and death. The main reason behind this event is to develop the willingness to donate blood among students.

WHITE CANE DAY


"Kindness is a language that deaf people can hear and blind people can see"
White cane day is a national observance day for the safety of blind people. In YRC we will throw light on them by collecting funds, celebrating it in blind schools or orphanages, conducting activities for them and presenting them some useful gifts.

PRAKNAI (Health Awareness Camp)


Praknai is a health awareness camp conducted by Youth Red Cross - CEG every year. Various health related competitions are also conducted for the students. The main reason behind this event is to stress the importance of healthy life among the students.

VIVEGA


"Spread smile everywhere"
A one day thematic event organized by the YRC Volunteersof CEG Campus, Anna University to commemorate Swami Vivekananda. The main objective of this event to bring out the inherent talents and interests of children from various Orphanage / Homes in and around Chennai.

NINAIVUGAL (Alumni Meet)


"Take only memories and leave foot prints"
Alumini meet is conducted each and every year to rewrite our bond with our seniors who lead and guide us towards the right path.

7 DAY SPECIAL CAMP


7 day special camp is conducted by Youth Red Cross CEG each and every year in a village for good exposure and for inculcating basic life experiences among the students under the banner health, service and friendship. Students will do activities for the upliftment of the village people.

AGARAM


Agaram is conducted in the month of october, every year to introduce YRC's activities, roles and responsibilities to the first year students.

THULIR THIRAN


Thulir Thiran a bedazzling event that aims at providing knowledge, throwing spotlight on innate talents and highlights the glory in service and your role as a YRC Volunteer.
